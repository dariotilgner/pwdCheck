	pwdCheck - password check
=================================

feel free to check the strength 
of your chosen password. at the 
moment the following criteria 
are in use to determine the 
actual strength of your password:

 * length
 * characters? 
   lower/upper case?
 * numbers
 * special characters?
 * repetitions of characters
